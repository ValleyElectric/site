ROBERT PEREZ grew up between Louisville and Boulder Colorado and now lives in Brighton. New to the Independent music scene, he is soon to release his debut concept album: Portraits of Emma Jane, which recounts the life of a woman who lived by the sea. He has previously worked as a piano bar musician and a-cappella singer in and around the Front Range.

THE VALLEY ELECTRIC is a group of the foremost Denver Metro musicians inculding: Adam Baerd (Guitar/Mandolin), Thomas Reis (Percussion), Duncan Britt (Electric Guitar), Eleanor Dunlap (Double Bass), Rachael Perez (Vocals) Avery Johnson(Cello), and Sam Martens (Vocals, Electric bass)
